Hover on the cards below to learn about different mushroom families

Agaricaceae: This is one of the largest families of mushrooms, and it includes the popular Agaricus mushrooms like the Agaricus bisporus (button mushroom), often found in grocery stores. Members of this family have gilled caps and are widely cultivated.

Boletaceae: The Boletaceae family is best known for its thick, fleshy mushrooms with pores rather than gills.

Russulaceae: The Russulaceae family contains mushrooms with a distinctive brittle texture. They are often found in woodlands and forests.

Tricholomataceae: This large family of mushrooms includes many species with white or cream-colored caps such as the Honey Mushroom
